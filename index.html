An undertaking of Conoscenza 2019

GENERAL
RULES

All participants must bring their registration slip and college ID card/bonafide certificate to be allowed to participate.

Participants shall be permitted to enter the campus only in proper dress code.

Team members must belong to the same college.

Registration closes at 8:30 AM.

Decorum must be maintained in accordance with the rules established by the college.

The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
